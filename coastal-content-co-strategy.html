Business Phases

The roadmap from zero to scale

Built on the principle: do one thing well, get proof, then expand. Don't launch 8 services at once.

1
Months 1–2
Foundation

Set up and land first client

Before selling anything, get the foundations right. Then focus on closing one paying client.

  • Register ABN (sole trader to start, Pty Ltd when revenue justifies)
  • Open dedicated business bank account
  • Get professional liability / PI insurance (~$500–$1,500/yr)
  • Website live at coastalcontentco.com.au
  • Complete prospect research (50 businesses across 5 batches)
  • Begin outreach to top 15 prospects (email + Instagram DM)
  • Close first paid client at $500–$800/month
  • Deliver first month of content — get first real testimonial with metrics
💡 Free pilot strategy: Offer one business a free 30-day trial. "I'll manage your Instagram and Google profile for free. If it works, you pay $500/month. If not, we shake hands." Get proof, get a testimonial, convert to paid.
2
Months 2–4
Proof of Concept

2–3 paying clients + first case study

Turn the free pilot into a paying client, then sign 1–2 more. Build your first real case study with actual numbers.

  • 2–3 paying clients at Starter tier
  • First case study with hard numbers (followers gained, reach, calls generated)
  • Replace placeholder testimonials on website with real ones
  • Finalise pricing (undercut competitors — research complete)
  • Begin word-of-mouth referral loop — ask every happy client for one referral
3
Months 4–8
Stack Services

Add services in order — don't rush

Once you have 3–5 stable paying clients, add services one at a time. Each new service upsells to existing clients first.

Add first: AI Lead Response
Connect their Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp to auto-respond to leads 24/7. Fastest ROI proof. Upsell to existing clients at $600–$1,200/month add-on.
Add second: Email Marketing
Monthly newsletters for hospitality and tourism clients. Low extra effort once content workflow is running. Add $300–$500/month to existing packages.
Add third: SEO Content
Blog posts and website copy optimised for local search. "Best cafe Busselton", "whale watching Dunsborough" etc. $500–$1,500/month add-on.
Add fourth: AI Sales Outreach (B2B)
Apollo.io + cold email automation for B2B clients (accountants, IT, consultants). Different audience, higher ticket. $2,000–$4,000/month.
4
Month 8+
Scale

Systems, team, and $10K+ MRR

Once you're at $10K+/month, stop doing everything yourself.

  • Hire a VA (Philippines-based: ~$600–$800/month) for content production and scheduling
  • Build free lead magnets (e.g. "South West Business Social Media Audit" tool)
  • Start publishing on LinkedIn and YouTube as "AI Marketing Expert in WA"
  • Expand client base across all of WA (not just South West)
  • White-label platform resell opportunity (GoHighLevel SaaS stream)

Outreach Strategy

Email + Instagram DM

Two channels, one goal — get a reply. The close happens in the conversation, not the message.

📧 Cold Email — formal pitch

Reference one specific gap. One genuine observation. Clear free offer. No pressure.

Subject: Quick question about [Business Name]'s Instagram

Hi [Name],

I'm V Fernando — I live locally in [Busselton/Dunsborough] and run a small content and marketing service for businesses in the area.

I noticed [SPECIFIC GAP — e.g. "your last Instagram post was 6 weeks ago" / "your Google profile has no photos"].

Happy to share a few free ideas for [Business Name] — no strings attached.

Worth a quick chat?

V Fernando

📱 Instagram DM — warm approach

Short and casual. Goal is a reply only — not a close. Compliment + specific gap + free offer.

Hey [Name]! Love what you're doing at [Business Name] — [GENUINE COMPLIMENT].

I help local businesses with social content and I noticed [SPECIFIC GAP — e.g. "you haven't posted in a few weeks"].

I've got a couple of quick ideas — would you be open to a chat? Totally free, no pressure. 🤙

✅ Personalisation checklist — before every send

  • Their actual name (check website, Instagram bio, Facebook)
  • One specific observation about their online presence
  • Correct business name spelling
  • Busselton OR Dunsborough — right location
  • One genuine compliment (DMs only)
  • Sign off as V Fernando — not a company name
