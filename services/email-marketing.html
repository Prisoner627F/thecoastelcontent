FAQ

Common questions

I don't have an email list yet. Can you still help?
Absolutely. We start by setting up the infrastructure and then help you grow your list — signup forms on your website, a QR code in-store, an incentive for subscribers, and prompts in your social content. Most businesses are surprised how quickly a list builds when you have a system for it.
How often will emails go out?
Monthly as a base. For seasonal businesses, we may increase frequency before major holidays or your peak season. We'll find the right cadence for your specific business and audience — too many emails causes unsubscribes, too few and people forget you.
What platform do you use?
We use GoHighLevel (included in your plan), Mailchimp, or Klaviyo — depending on your setup. If you already have an email platform, we can work within it.
Is email marketing included in the Starter plan?
Email marketing (monthly newsletter) is included in the Growth plan ($1,200/month). It can be added to the Starter plan as an add-on. Contact us to discuss.
