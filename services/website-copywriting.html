Real examples

The difference clear copy makes

❌ Before — Trades homepage
"Welcome to Mike's Plumbing. We are a family-owned plumbing business based in Busselton, Western Australia. We offer a wide range of plumbing services for both residential and commercial clients. Please contact us for a quote."
✓ After
"Busselton's most-called plumber for fast, reliable work. No call-out fee. Same-day service for urgent jobs. Licensed and insured — we've been fixing South West homes since 2009. Call now for a free quote →"
❌ Before — Tourism operator
"We offer whale watching tours from Dunsborough. Our experienced guides will take you out on the water to see whales in their natural habitat. Tours operate from June to November. Book online today."
✓ After
"Watch humpback whales breach just metres from your boat — guided by locals who've spent 15 years on these waters. 97% of our passengers see whales. Our small-boat format means 12 guests maximum, never a crowd. June to November only — spots sell out weeks ahead."
