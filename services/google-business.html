FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to give you my Google login?
No. We manage your profile through Google's Business Profile Manager using owner access. You simply add us as a manager — no sharing passwords. You retain full ownership and can remove access anytime.
What if I already have a Google profile set up?
Great — we start with a full audit of what's there and fix what's missing or incorrect. Most established profiles have significant gaps in categories, descriptions, attributes, and photo coverage that are easy wins for ranking improvement.
How long until I rank in the top 3?
For low-competition searches (specific town + service), improvements show within 2–4 weeks. For broader competitive terms, expect 2–3 months. We'll show you your current rankings and track progress monthly.
Can you help me get more reviews?
Yes — we create a review request strategy for you. This includes the right timing to ask, a short template message you can send or hand to customers, and a QR code that goes straight to your review page. We don't buy reviews or use any tactics that violate Google's terms.
Is this included in the Starter package?
Yes. Google Business Profile management is included in both the Starter ($500/month) and Growth ($1,200/month) packages. It's one of the highest-ROI things we do — often generating the first measurable results within the first month.
