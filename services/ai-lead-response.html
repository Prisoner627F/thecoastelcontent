FAQ

Common questions

Will it sound like a robot?
No — we spend time training it on your voice, your specific services, and how you naturally talk. Customers rarely know they're talking to AI. We'll show you examples during setup and adjust until it sounds right.
What if the AI gives wrong information?
We train it carefully with your exact pricing, availability, and FAQs. For anything it's unsure about, it's configured to say "let me get you a better answer" and alert you. We also review conversations weekly to improve accuracy.
Can it actually take bookings?
Yes, with the right setup. We connect it to your booking system (if you have one) or send payment links via GoHighLevel. The AI can confirm bookings, collect customer details, and send confirmation messages — all without you involved.
What channels does it work on?
Instagram DMs and comments, Facebook Messenger, Google Business messages, and website chat widget (if applicable). WhatsApp can also be added on Custom plans.
Is it available on the Starter plan?
AI Lead Response is available on the Growth plan ($1,200/month) and above. It can also be added as a standalone service. Contact us to discuss options for your specific situation.
