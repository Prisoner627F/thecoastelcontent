FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to provide photos or videos?+
Not always. We work with what you have — even if that's just photos on your phone. We'll tell you exactly what kinds of content to capture and make it as easy as possible. For Reels, we write the script and you film a 30-second clip.
Will I see content before it's published?+
Yes, always. You get a full month of content for approval before anything goes live. We use a shared Google Doc or Notion page so the review process is simple. You can approve, comment, or request changes — as many rounds as needed.
How many posts per week?+
Starter package: 3–5 posts per week. Growth package: 5–7 posts per week including Reels. We recommend daily posting to maximise algorithmic reach, but we tailor the volume to your business type and goals.
What platforms do you manage?+
Instagram is our core focus — it's the highest-ROI platform for South West businesses. We also post to Facebook automatically from Instagram. TikTok, LinkedIn, and Google Posts are available on Growth and Custom plans.
How long until I see results?+
Most clients see improved reach and engagement within 2–4 weeks. In-store mentions from Instagram typically start in week 4–6. Algorithm trust builds over 2–3 months, which is when follower growth accelerates significantly.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. We work month-to-month. Just give us 14 days notice before your next billing date. We're confident enough in our work that we don't need to trap you.
