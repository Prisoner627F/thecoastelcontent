FAQ

Common questions

Do I own the website once it's built? +
Yes — 100%. You own the domain, the hosting account, and all the files. We hand everything over on completion. There are no monthly platform fees to us, no lock-in contracts, and no strings attached.
What if I already have a domain and hosting? +
No problem. We can build on your existing setup or migrate you to a better host as part of the project. Either way, the final site goes where you want it.
Will I be able to update the site myself? +
Yes. We build on platforms that are easy to manage — you'll be able to update photos, change opening hours, and add new content yourself. We also include a short walkthrough so you know how it works.
Do I need to write the copy myself? +
No — that's part of what we do. We'll ask you a few questions about your business at the start, and we write everything from there. Most clients are surprised at how well we capture their voice without them having to write a single word.
How long does it take? +
Most sites are live within 2 weeks of the project starting. Larger or more complex builds may take 3–4 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline before we begin.
Can you integrate online booking? +
Yes. If you use a booking system (NowBookIt, Acuity, Calendly, or similar), we can embed it directly into your site. If you don't have one, we can recommend one that suits your business type.
