## PERSONALISATION CHECKLIST

Before hitting send on any message, confirm you have:

- [ ] Their actual name (check Google, website, Instagram bio, Facebook)
- [ ] One specific observation about their current online presence
- [ ] One genuine compliment about their business (DMs only)
- [ ] Correct business name spelled right
- [ ] Busselton OR Dunsborough — pick the right one for their location

---

## AUDIT NOTES TO LOOK FOR (when researching each business)

These are the gaps most likely to exist — note which apply to each prospect:

- Last Instagram post date (anything older than 2 weeks = gap)
- No Reels at all
- No Google Business Profile / incomplete profile
- No review responses
- Website looks outdated or has no clear contact/booking info
- Facebook active but Instagram dead (or vice versa)
- No email visible anywhere online
- Under 500 Instagram followers despite being established
- Poor quality photos (dark, blurry, no consistency)
- No link in bio or broken link
